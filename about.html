Learn About Us

LawyerUp is a law firm in New Delhi (India). We are proud of the reputation and respect we have earned as Delhi’s leading law firm. The depth and breadth of expertise we offer, combined with our ability to fulfil client’s needs and deliver individually tailored legal solutions, continue to position us at the forefront of cutting-edge legal services and ensure that we continuously surpass client expectations.

Our uniquely focused practice and years of combined experience and expertise in all aspects of law ensures that our client‘s interest is protected at any cost. Our significant experience and expertise in handling complex and high net worth cases help our client get the desired result.

We pride ourselves on our expertise and specialization in various field of law, our quality work, our commitment towards the interest of our clients and achieving positive and highly satisfactory results for our clients.

We are fully committed to offer each of our clients:
  1. Utmost personal attention.
  2. Prompt response to individual needs and queries.
  3. Creative solutions to difficult and complex problems.
  4. Educating clients in respect of legal processes.
  5. High quality of legal services including legal opinion/advice, drafting and court representation.
  6. Affordable and flexible fees structure.
  7. Complete confidentiality and Integrity towards our client.
  8. Earliest possible resolution of matter.
  9. Fullest satisfaction to our client at every stage of our services.
